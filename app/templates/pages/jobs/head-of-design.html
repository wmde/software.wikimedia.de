In order to promote these aims, we are currently seeking a capable and motivated Head of Design to join our Software & Development Department at the heart of Berlin to support the future growth of our Wikidata Platform. You will be responsible for our team of designers. This role will report to the Head of the department.

What we expect

As Head of Design you have:

  • 7+ years of experience in software industry
  • 3+ years of experience in managing a team
  • Experience in Product- or UX-Design
  • Excellent leadership skills
  • Comprehensive knowledge of design processes and methods
  • Ability to structure complex situations with multiple stakeholders to reach clear decisions

What you will perform

As Head of Design you are responsible for:

  • Keeping the organizational goals and user needs at the center of design decisions
  • Facilitating work on the UX of multiple products on the levels of research, concept interaction design and UI design
  • Representing the UX team in meetings and decisions making processes
  • Facilitating growth and learning for the UX team
  • Developing processes for distributing, sharing and communicating tasks in the team and between the UX team and other teams like engineering and community communication
  • Advocating User Centered Design
  • Owning and driving the design process by independently defining requirements, scoping projects and prioritizing tasks

What we offer

  • You can attend local user groups during work time and you are encouraged to do so
  • You will have a noticeable impact on one of the top four visited websites in the world
  • We regularly go to conferences, both as attendees and participants
